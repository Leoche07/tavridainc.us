About Tavrida Inc

A carrier built by people who understand the road. We move freight reliably across 48 states, with a team that treats every load like it's our own.

Our Story

Built on Experience. Driven by Integrity.

Tavrida Inc was founded in Mansfield, Ohio with a simple mandate: run a trucking operation we'd want to drive for ourselves. That meant building real relationships with shippers, paying owner-operators fairly, and refusing to cut corners on safety or service.

Today we operate within the freight networks of some of the largest names in American logistics — moving general dry van freight, temperature-controlled reefer loads, flatbed and step-deck specialty cargo, and providing power-only capacity where shippers need it.

Our growth has been deliberate. We hire experienced operators, maintain modern equipment, and back every load with around-the-clock dispatch. The result is a carrier that customers trust to deliver and drivers want to stay with.

Power Only

We provide the tractor — the shipper or broker provides the trailer. Power-only contracts give our operators steady, predictable work inside the country's largest freight networks without the overhead of trailer ownership.

  • Approved capacity for Amazon Relay, JB Hunt, USPS, UPS, and FedEx
  • Consistent lanes — fewer dead miles, more predictable income
  • Drop-and-hook operations at established freight hubs
  • 24/7 dispatch backup for breakdowns, detention, and reroutes

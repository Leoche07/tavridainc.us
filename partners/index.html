How We Work With Each Network

AMAZONRelay & DSP

Amazon Relay Network

We run loads through Amazon's Relay platform, moving freight between fulfillment centers, sort centers, and delivery stations across the country. Amazon Relay holds carriers to strict on-time and acceptance standards — standards we consistently meet.

JB HUNTDedicated Contract

JB Hunt Dedicated Capacity

JB Hunt is one of the most demanding shippers in North American freight. Our authorization to run inside their dedicated contract services means we meet their compliance, equipment, and service-level requirements — and that translates directly to other shippers.

USPSPostal Service

USPS Highway Contract Routes

Postal service lanes operate to fixed schedules with no tolerance for delay. Running USPS contract routes has built operational discipline into how we dispatch every load — postal-grade reliability, applied across the board.

UPSFreight & Ground

UPS Contract Operations

UPS contract work spans freight, ground, and surge capacity during peak seasons. We provide consistent equipment availability and trained operators who understand UPS's hub-and-spoke timing requirements.

FEDEXContract Carrier

FedEx Contract Carrier Services

Contracted capacity for FedEx Freight and FedEx Ground operations. We maintain the safety scores, insurance levels, and operational reliability required to keep this relationship active.

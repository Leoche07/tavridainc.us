About Tavrida Inc

A Carrier Built for the Long Haul

Tavrida Inc is a trusted interstate trucking company headquartered in Mansfield, Ohio. We deliver reliable freight solutions across the United States, combining operational efficiency with a genuine commitment to driver and customer success.

Our team understands the demands of the road from firsthand experience. From power-only partnerships with major shippers to specialized reefer and open deck hauls, we bring the right equipment, the right people, and the right attitude to every load.

What We Haul

Our Freight Services

We match the right equipment and expertise to every load — from ambient dry van freight to temperature-sensitive and oversized cargo.

Service 01

Power Only

We provide the tractor — you provide the trailer. Our power-only operations run within the networks of Amazon, JB Hunt, and USPS, giving drivers consistent lanes and stable income without trailer ownership overhead.

Service 02

Dry Van

General freight hauling in fully enclosed trailers. Suitable for palletized goods, consumer products, manufacturing components, and retail distribution — protected, on schedule, across 48 states.

Service 03

Reefer

Temperature-controlled transport for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and specialty goods. Maintaining precise conditions from pickup through final delivery is a standard we don't compromise on.

Service 04

Open Deck

Flatbed and step-deck solutions for oversized, heavy, or irregularly shaped loads. Construction materials, industrial machinery, and specialized equipment hauled safely and compliantly.
